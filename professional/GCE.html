Google Certified Educator

Issuer: Google for Education

As more and more classes have been moved to the online mode, educators should be creative in utilizing online tools in teaching, grading, and learning processes. The certification process of becoming a Google Certified Educator provides a great platform to foster innovation in integrating online tools in teaching. There are many google tools can be incorporated in remote classrooms to enhance the teaching quality and promote engagement. For example:

  • Google Classroom can be used to store assignments and class materials. It also has features to allow educators to post annoucements where students can respond to them.
  • Google Earth can be used to measure the area or length of a specific landmark.
  • Google Forms can be used to set interactive quizzes where they can be marked automatically. The forms can also be used to survey on people's opinions on different subjects.
  • Google Sites can be used to record everything in one place such as class syallabus, assignment deadlines, revision materials etc.

Those who are interested in becoming a Google Certified Educator can find further information on the website of Google for Education. The Level 1 Certification expects test takers to demonstrate fundamental knowledge in integrating Google tools for education purposes, while the Level 2 Certification would test on advanced technology integration skills in utilizing Google tools for education purposes.
