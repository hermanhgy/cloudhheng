Fluid Mechanics Lab

I was serving as a lab demonstrator during my M.Sc. program. The labs that I have taught were mainly in the area of fluid mechanics, including wind energy lab, which was performed outdoor, and flow measurement lab which was performed in a wind tunnel. The lab was designed to help students strengthen their understanding in the engineering subjects of thermodynamics and fluid mechanics.

Being a lab demonstrator during my M.Sc. program is also part of the reasons why I enrolled in a professional course - Philosophy and Practice of University Teaching to seek continuous opportunites for improvements in teaching.
