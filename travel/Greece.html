My Trip to Greece

Visited in: Jul 2022

After a long travel break due to COVID-19, I finally had the chance to travel again. This time, I went to Greece to attend the Committee on Space Research (COSPAR) conference in Athens, followed by a few days of summer vacation on Santorini Island. It was wonderful to travel once more and learn in this beautiful country that is so rich in culture, history, and warm hospitality.
