My Trip to Vatican City

Visited in: Jan 2018

Vatican City is the smallest country in the world, yet the city has the largest basilica in the world — St Peter's Basilica. It is common for people to visit this small but unique country while visiting Rome. Vatican City locates the headquarters of the Roman Catholic Church, and it is also the place where the Pope resides.


I was fortunate to be able to attend a Sunday mass celebrated by Pope Francis himself during my visit. This mass is normally extremely crowded due to the high popularity of the Pope, but the entrace is entirely free! Nonetheless, a (free) ticket needs to be booked in advance to attend this mass. More information about the ticket can be found on Papal audience official website.
